# *Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse*

## Overview
**Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse** is an *animated superhero film* produced by **Sony Pictures Animation** and **Marvel**.  
It tells the story of **Miles Morales**, a teenager who becomes the new Spider-Man in his universe.

---

### Plot Summary
> "Anyone can wear the mask."  
> — *Peter Parker*

After being bitten by a radioactive spider, **Miles Morales** develops spider-like powers.  
When **Kingpin** opens portals to other dimensions, different versions of Spider-Man team up to save the multiverse.

---

### Main Characters
- **Miles Morales** – The new Spider-Man.  
- **Peter B. Parker** – A mentor from another universe.  
- **Gwen Stacy** – A confident Spider-Woman.  
- **Kingpin** – The villain trying to bring back his family.

---

### What Makes It Special
1. **Unique Animation Style** – Looks like a living comic book.  
2. **Powerful Message** – Shows that *anyone can be a hero.*  
3. **Diverse Representation** – Brings a new and fresh hero into the Spider-Man legacy.
